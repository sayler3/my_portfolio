Burger-EaterMore

Deployed Repo

Burger-Eaterclose

Eat-The-Burger! is a restaurant app that lets users input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. Whenever a user submits a burger's name, the app will display the burger on the left side of the page -- waiting to be devoured. Each burger in the waiting area also has a Devour it! button. When the user clicks it, the burger will move to the right side of the page.The app will store every burger in a database, whether devoured or not.The user is also able to clear their table with delete functionality.
